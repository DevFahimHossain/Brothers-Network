Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refund procedures for Brother's Network services.

Policy Information

This policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds.

Effective Date

January 01, 2026

1

Refund Eligibility

Brother's Network provides refunds under specific circumstances. To be eligible for a refund:

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of the payment date.
  • Service must not have been used for more than 3 days from activation.
  • Technical issues must be verified by our support team as unresolvable.
  • Refunds are not available for partial months of service.
  • One-time setup or installation fees are non-refundable.
2

Non-Refundable Services

The following services and situations are generally non-refundable:

  • Monthly services that have been active for more than 7 days
  • Any promotional or discounted packages
  • Equipment purchases (modems, routers, cables, etc.)
  • Installation and setup fees
  • Services suspended due to policy violations
  • Payments made through third-party payment processors (subject to their policies)
3

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Contact Support

Reach out to our customer support team via phone, email, or in-person at our office to discuss your refund request.

Step 2: Provide Details

Have your account information, payment receipt, and reason for refund ready for verification.

Step 3: Verification

Our team will verify your eligibility based on our refund policy criteria.

Step 4: Processing

If approved, refunds are processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

4

Technical Issues & Service Problems

If you're experiencing technical issues with our service:

  • Contact our technical support team immediately for assistance
  • Allow our team 48 hours to resolve the issue before considering a refund request
  • Persistent unresolved issues may qualify for a prorated refund
  • Refunds for service issues are calculated based on downtime duration

Note: We strive to resolve all technical issues promptly. In most cases, technical problems can be resolved without needing a refund.
