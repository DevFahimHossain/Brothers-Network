Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing address, and payment information when you register for our services.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our network services, including connection times, data usage, and service preferences.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type for technical support and network optimization.
  • Communication Data: Records of your communications with our support team for quality assurance and training purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

Service Provision

To deliver and maintain our network services, process payments, and provide customer support.

Security & Protection

To protect against fraudulent activities, unauthorized access, and ensure network security.

Communication

To send important service updates, promotional offers (with consent), and respond to inquiries.

Analytics & Improvement

To analyze service usage patterns and improve our network performance and customer experience.
